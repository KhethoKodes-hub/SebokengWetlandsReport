Evidence‑based recommendations (32 surveys)

Immediate priorities

  • Solid waste & smell crisis: 75% dumping, 78% bad smells – deploy skips, daily collection near schools.
  • Sewer emergency response: 69% frequent overflows – fix infrastructure affecting schools.
  • Health screening: 47% report headaches/chest/lung issues – involve clinic and school health teams.

Engagement & education

  • Wetland literacy crash course: only 34% correct definition – integrate into school curriculum.
  • Monthly clean‑ups: 84% willing – launch “Drop by Drop” committee.
  • Fix reporting: 78% never reported – create simple WhatsApp line + follow‑up protocol.
