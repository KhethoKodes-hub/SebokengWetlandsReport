Wetland awareness

Only 54.5% correctly identified a wetland (water, plants, flood control). 36% unsure.

Residence near wetland

64% live within 100‑300m, 18% less than 100m – high exposure.

Length of stay

54.5% >10 years, 27% <1 year – mixed deep/long roots.

  • 54.5% sewer overflow

Recommendations based on 11 responses

Immediate priorities

  • Solid waste emergency: 73% report dumping – install skips, regular collection.
  • Sewer response unit: 73% frequent overflows – fast-track repairs.
  • Health screening: 5 households already reporting symptoms (headache, etc).

Engagement & education

  • Wetland literacy: only 54% know basic definition – school workshops.
  • Monthly clean‑ups: 9 residents willing – form “Drop by Drop” committee.
  • Report‑and‑fix hotline: 45% never reported – simplify process, follow up.
